The Method

It's a science,
not a shortcut.

Hankology started with one obsession: the smash. Hot griddle, hard press, maximum crust — every burger here is built on that same controlled reaction, then pushed into territory most fast-food kitchens won't touch.

That's where the Tarb Burger and the Tokyo Bite come in — fusion plates born from running Egyptian comfort food through global flavor labs, then handing the results to people who just want a really good burger, fast.
